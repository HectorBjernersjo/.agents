You will be given an architectural problem in this PR.

Your first goal is to look at the code and understand the problem deeply. When you have a good grasp on what the problem is, consider the different ways you could solve it.

Write the best 3-5 solutions to:

```text
docs/prs/<branch-slug>/reviews/<review-id>/architecture/<problem-slug>/suggested_solutions.md
```

Use a short, filesystem-safe `<problem-slug>` that describes the problem.

Make sure not to overcomplicate the solutions.

Then, one at a time, verify those solutions against the code. Check that they could actually be implemented, would compile, and would have a good impact on the codebase.

If a solution does not hold up, either fix it or remove it from the file.

When you have done all that, read:

```text
<architecture-steps-dir>/subagent-prompts/PICK_SOLUTION.md
```
